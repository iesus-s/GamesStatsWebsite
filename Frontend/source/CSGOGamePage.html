Our Recommended Players

s1mple

Oleksandr “s1mple” Kostyliev was born on October 2, 1997 and is currently inactive on the Natus Vincere roster. s1mple is considered by many to be the best Counter-Strike player of all time, a claim that is supported by his many achievements and trophies.

NiKo

Nikola “NiKo” Kovač was born on February 16, 1997 and is currently playing for G2 Esports as a rifler. NiKo is considered by many to be one of the best players in CS:GO, putting up fantastic statistics and known for, among other things, his Deagle plays.

m0NESY

Ilya “m0NESY” Osipov is a Russian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He is currently playing for G2 Esports as an AWPer. Born on May 1, 2005, he is considered one of the game’s greatest talents. He was invited to play in FPL (FACEIT’s highest PUG league, where professionals play pickup games) at just 14 years old.
