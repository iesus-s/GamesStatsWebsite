Our Recommended Players

Kyedae

Kyedae ‘Kyedae’ Shymko is a content creator and streamer who is currently focusing on Valorant.

ScreaM

Adil “ScreaM” Benrlitom is a professional VALORANT player from Belgium who is currently playing for Karmine Corp. ScreaM started his professional gaming career as a pro Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player, where he achieved great successes with teams such as G2, VeryGames, and EnVyUs.

yay

Jaccob “yay” Whiteaker is a professional VALORANT player from the United States of America who is currently playing for Bleed eSports. Before transitioning to VALORANT, he was a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player, where he is most known for his time in compLexity Gaming. He made the switch to VALORANT in September of 2020 when he signed for Andbox and later for Cloud9.

L1NK

Before becoming a professional VALORANT player, L1NK was active in the Counter-Strike”: Global Offensive scene, where he played for teams such as Vexed Gaming, London Esports, and Wind and Rain.
